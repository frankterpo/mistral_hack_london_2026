# 🎯 Quest Objective

Make sure you are at the root of Chapter 1 in your terminal:

`cd sample-once-upon-agentic-ai/1_strands_basics`

[
## Step 1: Enable the Sight Logging Spell 👁️

](#step-1:-enable-the-sight-logging-spell)
``1
`# TODO: Add debug logging to see what your agent is thinking`

Before summoning your agent, it's wise to enable the sight logging spell that lets you peer into your agent's mind. Check the documentation on how to do [debug logging](https://strandsagents.com/latest/documentation/docs/user-guide/observability-evaluation/logs/#configuring-logging) to help you.

There are different logs levels: `DEBUG`, `INFO`, `WARNING`, `ERROR`, and `CRITICAL`. You can adjust the log level to see what's happening behind the scenes. We recommend `INFO` as a starting point but feel free to adjust according to your expectations.

[
## Step 2: Forge Your Digital Companion 🤖

](#step-2:-forge-your-digital-companion)
``1
`#TODO: Create an Agent with the following system prompt: "You are a game master for a Dungeon & Dragon game"`

The system prompt is your agent's character sheet - it defines their personality, knowledge, and behavior. Check the documentation on how to create a [system prompt](https://strandsagents.com/latest/documentation/docs/user-guide/concepts/agents/prompts/).

[
## Step 3: Awaken Your Creation 🌟

](#step-3:-awaken-your-creation)
``1
`#TODO: Summon your agent with a basic query such as: `"Hi, I am an adventurer ready for adventure!"``

Time to breathe life into your creation! Check the documentation on how to [summon your agent](https://strandsagents.com/latest/documentation/docs/).

[
## Step 4: Testing Your Creation 🎲

](#step-4:-testing-your-creation)

Once you've completed all TODOs, run your script:

``1

If successful, your newly summoned Game Master should respond with enthusiasm, perhaps setting the scene for an epic D&D adventure!
